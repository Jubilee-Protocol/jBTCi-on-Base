# jBTCi FAQ

## What is jBTCi?
jBTCi is the first Bitcoin Index Fund on Base, providing diversified BTC exposure through a mix of cbBTC and WBTC.

## How does it work?
When you deposit cbBTC, you receive jBTCi tokens representing your share of the index fund. The fund automatically rebalances between cbBTC and WBTC to optimize returns.

## What is the minimum deposit?
The minimum deposit is 0.01 BTC.

## What is the deposit cap?
The current deposit cap is **1 BTC** as we launch in a controlled manner to ensure security and stability. As the protocol matures and undergoes additional auditing, we will progressively increase the cap toward our maximum of **1,000 BTC**. Cap increases will be announced on our Discord and Twitter.

## What are the fees?
- **Performance Fee**: 10% of profits only
- **Management Fee**: None
- **Deposit/Withdrawal Fee**: None (only gas costs)

## How do I get cbBTC?
You can swap ETH or other tokens for cbBTC on [Uniswap](https://app.uniswap.org/swap?chain=base&outputCurrency=0xcbB7C0000aB88B473b1f5aFd9ef808440eed33Bf).

## What is the expected APY?
The strategy targets 6-10% APY through yield optimization and rebalancing.

## Is jBTCi audited?
Yes! See our [Audit Report](./AUDIT_REPORT.md) for full details.

## What are the risks?
- Smart contract risk
- Oracle failure risk
- Market volatility
- Potential total loss of deposited funds

Please read our Terms of Service before depositing.

## Who governs jBTCi?
jBTCi is a product of Jubilee Protocol, governed by Hundredfold Foundation and developed by Jubilee Labs.

## How do I withdraw?
1. Go to the Withdraw tab
2. Enter the amount of jBTCi you want to redeem
3. Click "Withdraw cbBTC"
4. Confirm the transaction in your wallet
